What is a Stock 'n' Save event?

 

To encourage brand discovery and order writing, ANAM Craft hosts buying events throughout the year. The very first Stock 'n' Save event is coming in November, so make sure your brand's storefront is up to date with products, pricing, and new imagery!

 

Does this event require participation?

No discount or participation is required! We will be providing buyers with shopping promotions at no cost to you.

 

Share the news of this event and promote your products by sharing the promotion codes to encourage your buyers to shop your brand and save!
