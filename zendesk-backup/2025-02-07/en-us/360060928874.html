What is important to know as an Anam Maker?

With ANAM we offer a safe, centralized platform for buyers to find authentic makers, and we encourage the addition of beautiful craft and design from Celtic nations. To allow this marketplace to continue to expand our offerings and elevate our makers, you agree to the following:

  • Transactions are not directed off the ANAM platform, including reorders
  • Listing products on ANAM at the same prices as all other wholesale channels
  • Shipping charges to customer cannot exceed 40% of order total
  • Maintaining a prompt and reliable relationship with buyers 
  • Understanding and agreeing to ANAM’s Terms + Conditions
