How do I cancel or edit an order that has already been placed?

Please be mindful that many products are crafted and made specific to orders that are received by the Maker. Upon placing an order on ANAM Craft, you have 24 hours to contact the Maker or our Support Team to cancel an order.

How to cancel:

  • Send a message to the Maker from your Message Center, or by using the Message the Maker button on their storefront
  • Provide some detail on reason for cancellation

How to edit:

  • If you have ordered a product(s) inadvertently, you have 24 hours to message the Maker to remove that product(s)
  • If you forgot to add products to your order, you must submit a new order at this time
