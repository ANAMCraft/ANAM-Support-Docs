How to Select Categories (Limit to 3) for Your Product

Select up to 3 categories that directly pertain to your item.

For example, a Claddagh pendant should have the primary “Necklaces and Pendants - Jewelry - Type” category. This should also be listed in “Claddagh - Jewelry - Style" category.

                                                                                                                                                         

*Please note: All products in a sub-category will automatically show if a buyer is looking at the parent category, so there’s no need to select all. That is, the above categories for "Type" and "Jewelry" do not need to be selected. If a buyer chooses to look through all Jewelry, all products from sub-categories will display.
