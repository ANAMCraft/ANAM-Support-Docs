What is an ANAM Maker Market and how do I participate?

ANAM Maker Markets are exciting 3 or 4 day online events for wholesale buyers and vendors to connect and trade, with brand discounts on offer and exclusive promotions for buyers offered by ANAM Craft!

 

The next Maker Market:

Monday August 28th to Thursday August 31st

 

 

 

Why participate?

It's a great opportunity build brand awareness and connect with new and existing buyers. Plus, ANAM Makers benefit as well!

How are the benefits to participating Makers (Sellers)?

All new orders are at a 5% reduced commission, and all "invited buyers" are 0%...completely commission-free.

How do I sign up and offer a discount?

Visit your Maker Portal and look for "Maker Markets" on the left side-bar. Follow the prompts on the following page, and don't forget to Save Event Details!
