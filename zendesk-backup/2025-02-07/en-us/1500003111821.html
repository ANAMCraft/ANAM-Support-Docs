Setting up your Buyer Profile

 

To sign in to your Buyer Account you must first be approved.  Once you have received your approval email you can follow the link from there or visit the ANAM Craft homepage and click the "Buyer Sign in" button at the top of the page.

Upon first sign-in, you will need to complete your profile by adding your store information, shipping address, and reseller/sales tax ID.

In your Buyer Dashboard you can also change your password, view order history, invoices, messages, and apply for 60-day net terms!
