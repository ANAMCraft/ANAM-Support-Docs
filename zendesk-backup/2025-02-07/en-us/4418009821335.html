How to view your home currency in the Maker Portal

Since ANAM Craft is $USD-based, the default currency in the Maker Portal shows in $USD.

When logged into your Maker Portal, to view your home currency, such as Euro:

1. Click on the profile icon in the top-left corner of your screen:

2. Hover over "US Dollars ($)" and choose your preferred currency:

     -->    

 

You will then view the chosen currency* alongside the $USD price wherever there is an amount or cost field. The image below is what the conversion you will see in your "All Products" tab.
