Where are the Makers and products located?

The sellers on ANAM, called Makers, are located in the Celtic nations of Ireland, Scotland, Wales, as well as the USA. The products made by ANAM Makers are crafted and composed in rich history, heritage, and passion. The location of every Maker is found on their individual storefront under their profile photo as well as the inspiration behind their products.

You can also look for the "Designed In" and "Made In" fields on product pages for this transparent detail.
