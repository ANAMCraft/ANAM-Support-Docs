When do I get paid?

When an approved retail buyer places an order, the payment details are captured and held until order fulfillment. Once the order is fulfilled, shipped, and marked as Complete in your Maker Portal the payment is released. Your payout is initiated, and will be deposited into your account within approximately 21 days*

 

To view your estimated payout date, visit Payout History on the left side-bar of your Maker Portal.

 

 

*International payouts may vary and can take an additional 3 business days
