What is the return policy?

Returns are available for your first order when shopping with a new Maker (reorder with a Maker are not eligible).

  • Return requests to be emailed to support@anamcraft.com
  • You are responsible for return shipping costs at this time
  • Customized products are not eligible, and all returns must be unused and in perfect condition
  • We cannot accept returns for testers or sample items
  • Shipping costs to your store are non-refundable.
