Submitting an order

 

Once you are signed in to your Buyer Profile you may shop from products across multiple makers. You will be able to view the Wholesale Price for each product and add to your cart. Each Maker's storefront has a Minimum Order Amount, and once this is reached you may check out with that Maker.

Your payment details will be stored in your profile, and you may manage your invoices from this area to see order history and easily place reorders.
