Shipping an Order

When fulfilling an order, you have the option to choose the shipping method preferred. Customers are promised at checkout that the shipping cost will not exceed 40% of the order total. You will add in this shipping cost during Step 1 of the order fulfillment process.

 

STEP 1: Edit Order/Add Shipping

       

               

Once the order is received and you are ready to ship, you will proceed through the steps in your Maker Portal. Please remember to "Complete" the order by entering the carrier and tracking details.
