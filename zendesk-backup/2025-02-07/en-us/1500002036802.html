Image Size Requirements

We recommend using web-optimized images in your store.

When uploading products we recommend a square size image, approx 1080x1080, appears the best.

The image should not be larger than 3000px by the largest side. The image size on the disk should not exceed 1-2 MB.

 

For your Maker storefront banner we recommend a size of 1240 × 380.

All other images do not have a specific size requirement, but will need to be of good quality.

If you are interested in the ANAM Craft team performing a bulk upload,  please reach out to makers@anamcraft.com for more information on this.
