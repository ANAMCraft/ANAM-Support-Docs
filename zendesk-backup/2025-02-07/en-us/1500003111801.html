Do I need a reseller or tax ID?

Yes, a Reseller ID is necessary to purchase wholesale goods on ANAM Craft marketplace. Upon first login, you will add this tax ID to your profile.

ANAM Craft Marketplace is a public facing website that allows everyone to view and enjoy the many beautiful, crafted products on the site. However, we are a trade-only platform where we vet our retailers prior to authorizing any purchases.
